The kitchen app that actually knows what's inside your fridge.
KAI Kitchen AI is a smart assistant designed to stop food from wasting away in your kitchen. Instead of making you manually type out every single grocery item you buy, it uses AI to scan your food and receipts, tracks when things expire, and cooks up recipes using only what you actually have at home.

🔥 What it does
1. The Dual-Option Scanner
Let's face it: scanning grocery items one by one is exhausting. KAI Kitchen AI gives you two choices depending on your grocery haul:

Whole Receipt Dump: Take a photo of your entire grocery receipt. The AI reads the messy store text (like turning ORG BNN into Organic Banana), ignores random things like taxes or grocery bags, and automatically guesses how long each food item will last.

Single Item Close-Up: If you just bought a few specific items or fresh produce without barcodes, snap a direct picture. The AI will look at the food, check its ripeness, and set a perfect expiration date.

2. A Shopping List That Actually Helps
This isn't a static notepad where you manually type everything out. It connects directly to your kitchen's inventory:

Smart Suggestions: If you ask KAI to make a dish like lasagna, but you only have chips and yogurt, it won't just say "no." It will tell you what you can make right now plus automatically add the missing lasagna ingredients straight to your Shopping List.

Auto-Clear: When you go to the store, buy those missing ingredients, and scan your new receipt, the app instantly notices, crosses them off your shopping list, and adds them right into your pantry.

3. Smart Recipe Logic
The app looks at what you have before it talks to you. If you tell it you want to make a snack out of cookies and ice cream, it knows not to tell you to "boil" or "bake" it. It limits the recipe to actions like crush, layer, slice, and freeze so your food suggestions always make sense.

🛠️ The Setup
Frontend: Simple, fast HTML, CSS, and plain JavaScript (no complex frameworks needed).

Backend: Node.js, Express, and a fast, local SQLite database.

AI: Gemini API for smart text reading and image analysis.
